About

Focused. Proven. Built for Speed.

Kore Power Solutions was founded to solve a growing problem in the U.S. electrical infrastructure market — long and unpredictable transformer lead times. We are a new company, actively building relationships with utilities, contractors, and developers across the country.

Our manufacturing partner has been producing transformers since 1984, including work alongside the United States Army Corps of Engineers. Their products are built to perform in demanding environments where reliability matters.

In the past year, these transformers have obtained UL listing for the United States and have been independently tested by U.S. engineers across more than 20 units to verify performance and build quality.

Kore Power Solutions was built to bring these proven transformers to the U.S. market with a simple focus: deliver faster, reduce hassle, and provide a higher level of support.

We are a straightforward, new company. We focus on one product, communicate clearly, and are committed to giving every customer a level of attention that larger distributors simply can't provide.
